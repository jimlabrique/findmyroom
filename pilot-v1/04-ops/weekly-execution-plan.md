# Weekly Execution Plan (4 weeks)

## Week 1 - Setup and first supply

## Goals

- Forms and Google Sheets fully operational
- Scoring engine validated on scenarios
- First 5 qualified colocs

## Deliverables

- Google Forms x2 live
- Google Sheets workbook with tabs, formulas, and filter views
- Ops templates ready (WhatsApp, shortlist, GDPR)
- 5 colocs status moved to `coloc_qualified`

## Week 2 - Reach full supply and start matching

## Goals

- Reach 10 qualified colocs
- Start candidate intake and first shortlists
- Keep SLA under 48h

## Deliverables

- 10 colocs in pipeline
- Candidate flow active
- First shortlist batch sent

## Week 3 - Operate, learn, stabilize

## Goals

- Maintain daily matching cadence
- Standardize feedback collection from colocs
- At most one scoring weight adjustment

## Deliverables

- Stable shortlist quality output
- Feedback collection routine in place
- Updated scoring rubric only if justified by data

## Week 4 - Close sample and decide

## Goals

- Reach sample target (10/60)
- Consolidate KPI and apply go/no-go gate
- Prepare post-pilot MVP handoff if GO

## Deliverables

- Completed KPI tracker
- Final decision note (GO or NO-GO)
- MVP handoff draft (if GO)
